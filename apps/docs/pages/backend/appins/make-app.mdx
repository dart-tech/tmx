# Make an app

Applications in the platform are a collection of Data Entities, Workflows, Connectors and Settings.

## Entities

Create entities/tables and configure them to meet your specific needs. Each data model has properties,
which are form of schema types that allow storing data in different forms.

## Workflows

You can use Workflows to automate tasks and processes in your app.

## Connectors

Connect to external services and APIs to extend the functionality of your app.
